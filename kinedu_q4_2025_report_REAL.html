Executive Summary

Q4 2025 demonstrated strong growth momentum with revenue reaching $1.20M (+13.9% QoQ) driven by exceptional subscription volume growth to 25,072 new subscriptions—a 24.1% increase from Q3. The quarter was characterized by improving unit economics with CAC declining to $24.21 (best of year) while maintaining healthy ARPU of $52.52. Marketing spend increased strategically to $607K, yielding positive returns through volume acceleration.

Key Performance Highlights:

  • Volume Surge: 25,072 new subscriptions in Q4 (+24% QoQ), with conversion rates climbing to 7.88% (up from 6.73% in Q3)
  • CAC Optimization: Average Q4 CAC of $24.21 represents 7% improvement QoQ and 26% improvement vs Q1
  • Revenue Momentum: Full year 2025 revenue reached $3.67M with strong Q4 finish representing 32.7% of annual revenue
  • Profitability Path: Contribution margin of $189K (15.7%) shows improving unit economics as scale increases

Areas Requiring Attention:

  • ARPU Stability: While healthy at $52.52 in Q4, ARPU down slightly from Q2-Q3 peaks of $54.72; maintaining pricing power critical
  • Gross Margin Pressure: 66.4% in Q4 down from 67% in Q1; need to monitor COGS as scale increases
